Books by Cameron West

Publications

Cameron West is the author of two books — a landmark memoir that changed how the world understands Dissociative Identity Disorder, and a sweeping historical thriller set in Renaissance Italy.

★ New York Times Bestseller

First Person Plural

My Life as a Multiple — 25th Anniversary Edition

Memoir  ·  Blackstone Publishing  ·  May 26, 2026

Described as "a page-turning journey through hell" by Entertainment Weekly, this 25th Anniversary Edition is a riveting, spellbinding memoir that boldly illuminates the truth behind Dissociative Identity Disorder and reveals the deep humanity behind the condition.

Originally published in 1999, it became a New York Times, Publishers Weekly, and USA Today bestseller — reaching #1 on Amazon and Barnes & Noble and sold in 22 countries. This new edition features a powerful Epilogue presenting the first physiological evidence of alter personalities via EEG brain wave patterns, along with an overview of current research on a condition now known to affect an estimated 100 million people worldwide.

Historical Thriller

The Medici Dagger

A Novel

Fiction  ·  Ballantine Books  ·  2001

A gripping historical thriller set against the opulent and treacherous world of Renaissance Italy. When a priceless dagger commissioned by the Medici family resurfaces after five centuries, it sets off a deadly race across continents — drawing together art, obsession, and murder in a novel that moves with the pace of a thriller and the richness of a history.

Cameron West's fiction debut showcases the same vivid, fearless storytelling that defined his memoir — this time in a world of intrigue, beauty, and mortal consequence.
