DID & Trauma

Resources

A curated selection of organizations offering information and support focused on trauma-based conditions such as dissociative disorders.

Professional Organizations

Traumatic Stress Institute

A nonprofit of international scope helping people understand, recover from, and treat traumatic stress including PTSD, dissociative disorders, and co-occurring issues such as addictions and self-injury.

ISST-D

The International Society for the Study of Trauma and Dissociation — a non-profit professional association promoting comprehensive, clinically effective and empirically based resources and responses to trauma and dissociation.

  • Find a Professional

Online Information, Support & Forums

An Infinite Mind

A 501(c)3 non-profit dedicated to educating the public and professional community about trauma-based dissociation with a primary focus on DID — working to dispel myths and stigmas.

Male Survivor

A leader in improving resources and support available to male survivors of all forms of sexual abuse — built on a foundation of respect and mutual partnership between survivors and professionals.

Pandora's Project

A nonprofit providing support and resources to survivors of rape and sexual abuse and their friends and family — offering online support groups, message boards, and informational resources.

Mosaic Minds

Founded in 1999 by dissociative survivors of childhood trauma — an online clearinghouse of information for those whose lives are impacted by Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID).

UK Based

First Person Plural UK

Specializes in working for and on behalf of all those affected by Dissociative Identity Disorder and similar complex trauma-related dissociative identity conditions.

PODS — Positive Outcomes for Dissociative Survivors

Offers a helpline, Find-a-Therapist resource, and professional training programs for therapists treating dissociative disorders.

Crisis Hotline

RAINN

Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network — the nation's largest anti-sexual violence organization, operating the National Sexual Assault Hotline in partnership with more than 1,000 local service providers.
